The African Dream That Became Reality

StageLink was born from a simple, frustrating reality that I faced as an African artist. Too many times, I watched incredible talent from my continent miss global opportunities because of broken systems, unnecessary gatekeepers, and geographical barriers.

As both a musician performing under the stage name UD and a tech enthusiast, I saw an opportunity to bridge worlds. Late nights coding in Lagos, between studio sessions and performances, the vision for StageLink took shape ‚Äî a platform where talent is the only currency that matters.
